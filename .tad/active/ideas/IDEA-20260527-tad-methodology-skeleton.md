## Summary & Problem

ECC's Hermes operator shell proves that "AI skill systems" can extend beyond software development (content, sales, finance, research). But ECC's approach is a flat skill library — no enforced process, no quality gates, no knowledge accumulation. TAD's competitive advantage is exactly this process backbone. To make TAD a general methodology, the approach should be: extract the Alex/Blake dual-agent + 4-Gate + knowledge accumulation skeleton as a domain-agnostic framework, then let Domain/Capability Packs carry domain-specific knowledge. The skeleton (Socratic inquiry → expert review → quality gates → knowledge capture) works for any complex decision domain, not just software.
