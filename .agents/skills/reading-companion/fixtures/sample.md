# The Craft of Reading

Reading well is a skill, not a reflex. The careful reader argues with the page.

## On Attention

Attention is the scarcest resource. A wandering mind absorbs nothing, however
many words pass before the eyes.

Sustained focus is built, paragraph by paragraph, like a muscle.

### A Note on Distraction

Notifications are the enemy of depth. Silence them.

## On Synthesis

Understanding is not collection. The reader must connect, compare, and rebuild
the argument in their own words.
